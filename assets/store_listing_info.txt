Passed Note — Handwritten Class Note Watch Face

(Alternates: "Passed Note — Notebook Paper Watch Face" /
 "Passed Note: A Note Passed To You In Class")


------------------------------------------------------------------------
FIELD: Description (Max 4000 characters)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
Passed Note turns your watch into a note somebody slipped you in class. The screen is a scrap of lined notebook filler paper — light-blue rules, a red margin line, punch holes hugging the left curve — and everything on it is handwritten in a different kid's handwriting: blue and black ballpoint, with the occasional red gel pen. Every line of writing sits right on the notebook rules, like the real thing.

FEATURES & DETAILS
• FOUR REAL HANDWRITINGS: The date is printed neatly in black pen, the time is written HUGE in loopy script with an emphatic squiggle underline, the stats are quick scrawl and someone else's cursive — four genuine handwriting typefaces, baked into crisp bitmap fonts sized for your exact screen.
• DOODLED LIVE WEATHER: Between the date and the time, the day's weather is sketched in pen strokes — a sun, sun-behind-cloud, cloud, rain, snowflake, or a storm cloud with a red lightning bolt — matching the live conditions, with the temperature beside it and the condition word ("sorta sunny", "rainy", "storms!") in blue cursive underneath.
• YOUR STATS, WRITTEN LIKE NOTES: Steps in a quick black scrawl with your heart rate doodled in red beside a little heart; device battery in blue cursive next to your Body Battery ("energy: 87%"); calories and your phone's notification count ("3 msgs!") squeezed onto the last rule.
• NOTEBOOK PAPER, DRAWN FOR YOUR SCREEN: The paper, rules, margin, punch holes, squiggles, and doodles are all drawn procedurally and scaled to your panel — no stretched bitmaps, crisp on every case size.
• GRACEFUL WHEN DATA IS MISSING: Weather, Body Battery, and heart rate hide cleanly when your watch has no data — the note just gets a little shorter.

PICK YOUR PEN & PAPER (SETTINGS)
• Time Pen — was the time written in blue ballpoint, black ballpoint, or red gel pen?
• Paper — classic white notebook filler paper, or a yellow legal pad.
• Show Weather — toggle the doodled weather block.
• Show Heart Rate — toggle the heart doodle and pulse.

ALWAYS-ON FRIENDLY
On AMOLED watches the always-on display switches to a burn-in-safe page — a black screen with just the time in dim ink, nudged a few pixels each minute. MIP / Solar panels always show the full note.

PRIVACY
Passed Note uses only the SensorHistory permission to read Body Battery. It does not collect or transmit any data off your watch.

SUPPORTED DEVICES
Connect IQ 4.0+ round Garmin watches — Forerunner (165, 255, 265, 570, 955, 965, 970), fenix 7/8 (incl. Solar) & fenix E, epix 2 / Pro, enduro 3, Venu 2/3 (& Venu 4), Vivoactive 5/6, AMOLED Instinct 3 / Crossover, plus Approach (golf), Descent (dive), D2 (aviation), and MARQ specialty watches. Monochrome MIP Instincts and square/rectangular panels are not supported — white paper with blue and black ink needs a round colour display.

Check yes if you like it. ☑

Small layout fixes so every line stays on the page:
